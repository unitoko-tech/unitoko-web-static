The Harsh Reality for Physical Retailers

Over 80% of India’s retail stores still operate offline. But they face hidden battles every day. Here are just a few.

Low Digital Visibility

Most small retailers lack online presence, making them nearly invisible to new-age customers.

Unfair Online Competition

Competing with giants who undercut prices and control the algorithms is a losing game.

Delivery & Fulfillment Gaps

Lack of structured delivery partners limits reach and causes poor buyer experiences.

Limited Customer Loyalty

Retailers struggle to retain or reward customers beyond in-store interactions.

No Tools for Smart Selling

Offers, campaigns, seasonal bundling — most stores have no tools to do this easily.

Operational Complexity

Managing stock, pricing, staff, and customer engagement manually is overwhelming.

Why Shopping at Local Stores Feels Hard

We love our neighborhood shops. But let’s be honest — buying from them often feels outdated and uncertain.

Can't Find Nearby Stores Online

Even in 2025, most local stores are missing from Google, maps, or shopping platforms.

Outdated or Inaccurate Listings

You finally find a store—only to discover the products listed are unavailable or mispriced.

Long Waits & Poor Fulfillment

No reliable delivery system leads to delays, failed orders, or unclear pickup times.

Hard to Discover Stock.

Nearby stores lack searchable catalogs, making it difficult to find exact items quickly.
